Contract-level star ratings, projected ratings, domain flags, and bonus exposure.

Plan metadata, domain performance, measure scorecards, and historical ratings.

HEDIS measure scorecard with numerator, denominator, open gaps, and status.

CAHPS rating, composite scores, survey window, gap to target, and QBP at stake.

Pulse wave response rates, top-box scores, official alignment, and confidence.

vw_intervention_hub

Process interventions, owning departments, lift, status, and due dates.

vw_team_view

Department ownership, team leader contact, metrics, required actions, and status.

vw_member_gap_list

Member-level open gaps, propensity, priority, recommended channel, and incentive.

vw_campaign_roi

Campaign performance, closures, cost per closure, estimated lift, and ROI.

vw_alerts_priorities

Critical alerts, warning signals, opportunity alerts, owner, and recommended action.
