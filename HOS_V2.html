Urgency Engine
How the agent scores each of the 302 open-gap members to determine who gets called first
How it works
The agent assigns a composite urgency score (0–100) to each of the 302 members with an open fall risk gap. Care management works the list from highest score down, ensuring the most at-risk members are contacted first. The score is recalculated nightly as new claims and EHR data flows in.

Homebound status5%Whether member is identified as homebound — needs different intervention pathway (home safety assessment)Claims · ICD-10 Z74.09Active homebound status = max points + routes to home safety pathway
How the score is calculated
Urgency Score = (Fall severity × 0.25) + (No AWV × 0.20) + (Age score × 0.15) + (Comorbidity score × 0.15) + (Mock survey score × 0.10) + (Visit gap × 0.10) + (Homebound × 0.05)
All sub-scores normalized to 0–100 before weighting. Final score rounded to nearest integer.
